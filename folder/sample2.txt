USA and Russia are two of the world's largest and most influential countries. The United States of America (USA) is located in North America, while Russia spans Eastern Europe and northern Asia, making it the largest country by land area. Both nations have significant roles in global politics, economics, and military affairs. Historically, they were rivals during the Cold War era, leading to significant developments in technology, space exploration, and international relations. Today, the relationship between the USA and Russia continues to impact global events.
